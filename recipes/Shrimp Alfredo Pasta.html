Shrimp Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Shrimp Alfredo Pasta

Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and delicious dish that combines succulent shrimp with fettuccine pasta in a rich Alfredo sauce. It's a perfect meal for seafood lovers!. This dish is not only easy to prepare but also incredibly satisfying. The creamy sauce, made with heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, coats the pasta and shrimp beautifully, creating a comforting and indulgent meal. Whether you're cooking for a special occasion or just a weeknight dinner, Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
